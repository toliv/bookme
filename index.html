Your one-stop shop for booking and managing performing artists

Bookme is an online collaborative marketplace and management service, enabling event hosts to book and manage performing artists.

Find Out More

For Event Organizers

Bookme provides a convenient, collaborative marketplace to find, hire and manage artists and upcoming events.

For Artists

Bookme allows for a unique way to promote your brand, reach out to a large audience, find new clients or events to perform at, manage upcoming shows and get paid in full, securely and on time.

How It Works

For Event Promoters

Find Artists

With Bookme, you’ll be able to find the perfect artist for your concert, show, or any live event

Hire Artists

We make the booking process simple: after a request is sent to an artist and they approve the artist is reserved

Manage Logistics

Say goodbye to the headache of artist logistics! You’ll be able to work through every aspect of the booking process through Bookme

Pay Easily

With our secure payment processor, both you and the artist will be able to rest easy knowing your money is 100% secure

For Artists

Find Opportunities

Find amazing event opportunities: Thinking of Doing in Ibiza? Signing in San Francisco? Now it’s easier than ever to find gigs that interest you

Get Hired

Bookme does the heavy lifting regarding dealing with event contracts and logistics so you can spend more time performing

Manage Contracts

No more stressing that the host won’t pay you in full or worrying if transportation is organized. You’ll be able to track exactly what the host has left to fulfill for the event

Get Paid

Every event is protected by “Bookme Payment Protection” - ensuring that you will get paid for your performance
